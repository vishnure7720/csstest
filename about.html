My mission is to help you find an amazing wedding photographer or videographer for your day.

Someone that is not only going to get amazing images for you, but also that is going to mix in well with your day. I want you to be able to relax and enjoy your day to the fullest, trusting that after the day is done you are going to have this amazing record of all those special moments with your loved ones, all the small details you spent so much time choosing, and most importantly moments with your new love.



Why I started the company

I founded OREGON Weddings back in 2013 shortly after I had moved to London from Vancouver Canada. I had already been a photographer for over 10 years, photographing fashion, and portraits for magazines, working with some big celebrity photographers like ‘Bryan Adams’ and magazines like “Marie Claire” and “Elle”. I had photographed a few weddings back in Vancouver and was starting to photograph the occasional wedding in London.

It was at this time friends were starting to get married and planning their weddings. I would always get asked to help them find and book a photographer or videographer for their day.

Who Are Our Photographers?

I love our photographers!
some of our photographers have been with the company since we started. They began as assistants and now have thriving wedding photography businesses of their own and are some of our most booked photographers. We have award winning photographers and videographers that have been published in magazines and blogs.

We have photographers that shoot in Vintage, Classic, FineArt and Photojournalistic styles

And we also have new talented photographers joining us all of the time. I still think one of my best skills is finding photographers that are super talented but just starting out. These photographers are great at shooting smaller weddings like registry office weddings and because the photographers are still building their portfolios we can give our clients a great rate for their services. It’s a WIN/WIN for everyone.
